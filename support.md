# Support ASCII VJ Remix

ASCII VJ Remix is local-first creative software for DJs, VJs, media artists, and people building useful tools outside the usual platform economy.

Your support helps fund the work that makes the app better for live sets: smoother visuals, stronger output windows, better capture support, reliable releases, and more usable controls.

## What support funds

- renderer and output-window improvements
- audio-reactive visual control
- camera and local media workflow polish
- macOS signing and release maintenance
- Windows/Linux packaging verification
- accessibility, performance, and localization improvements
- presets and controls that are useful in real live sets

## Not ready to pay?

You can still help by trying releases, filing precise bugs, sharing useful presets, and bringing the tool to artists who will actually use it.
